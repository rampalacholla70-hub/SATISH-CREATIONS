THE HANDS BEHIND THE COFFEE
Written by
Satish Writings
A Satish Writings Production
SCENE 1
EXT. CITY STREET — MORNING
A peaceful morning. The city slowly wakes up. Sunlight falls between tall buildings. People briskly walk towards their workplaces.
A small, inviting coffee shop stands quietly on the corner of the street. A vintage board outside reads: "MOONLIGHT COFFEE".
Soft, melancholy piano music begins to play as the camera slowly moves towards the storefront window.
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — MORNING (DAY 1)
The entry door opens with a crisp DING! of the bell.
ARJUN (25) enters. A heavy laptop bag hangs off his shoulder. His eyes are fixed entirely on his smartphone screen, reading work messages as he walks forward.
He reaches the front counter blindly. Without looking up from his screen, he orders:
ARJUN
One regular coffee.
The cashier nods. Arjun continues scrolling intensely on his phone.
Behind the counter, someone starts preparing his beverage. The camera completely hides the barista's face, focusing strictly on her hands.
A beautiful pair of hands holds the porcelain coffee cup. A simple, elegant silver bracelet shines cleanly on her wrist.
The coffee is placed gently down on the counter. Arjun picks it up, his gaze still glued completely to his screen.
ARJUN
Thank you.
He turns and leaves. The camera remains fixed on the barista's hands as he exits.
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — MORNING (DAY 2)
A different morning. Arjun enters wearing a dark black hoodie, but his routine remains identical. His eyes are locked onto his smartphone.
ARJUN
One regular coffee.
Again, the same hands prepare his drink with care. The silver bracelet glints under the warm pendant lights. She places the cup down. Arjun grabs it immediately.
ARJUN
Thank you.
He leaves. The girl behind the counter watches his retreating figure. A small, faint smile appears on her face.
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — MORNING (DAY 3)
Another day. Arjun wears a sharp white shirt. He enters while typing quickly on his phone, completely oblivious to his surroundings.
ARJUN
Regular coffee.
Close-up framing: Rich coffee beans grinding, dark espresso pouring, hot steam rising over the metal tray. The silver bracelet moves elegantly as she finishes the cup.
She slides the cup into place.
ARJUN
Thank you.
He walks away. The barista stares after him. She realizes that while he has never once looked up to see her face, he has never missed saying those two exact words.
SCENE 2
EXT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — MORNING (DAY 4)
Arjun arrives at the coffee shop at the usual time, but the street feels unusually quiet today.
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — CONTINUOUS
The shop is exceptionally peaceful with only a few distant customers. The main service counter looks entirely empty.
Arjun looks around, visibly confused when he doesn't spot the usual presence. A loud work notification pings on his phone. He sighs heavily, walks to a corner table near the window, and opens his laptop.
The rapid sound of mechanical typing fills his quiet corner.
SCENE 3
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — LATER
The low hiss of the espresso machine starts up. SHHHHH.
Arjun keeps typing, lost in his work. Gentle footsteps approach his table. A soft shadow falls across his keyboard.
A perfect coffee cup is placed right in front of him. Then, a second cup is placed down right across from it. Two coffees.
Arjun stops typing. He stares at the two cups, confused. Slowly, his gaze traces up toward the hands.
The same delicate hands. The same silver bracelet.
His expression softens. The background music shifts into a tender, emotional piano track. The camera tracks smoothly upward: from the hands, past the sleeve, over the shoulder, up to her face.
It is ANANYA (23). She smiles down at him warmly.
ANANYA
Your regular coffee. Less sugar... Extra hot.
Arjun blinks in surprise. A small, realization-filled smile breaks across his face.
ARJUN
Wait... How do you know my coffee?
ANANYA
(smiling)
Because you order the exact same thing every day.
ARJUN
But... I never told you how I like it.
Ananya gently pulls out the chair and sits down across from him.
ANANYA
You didn't have to.
SCENE 4
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — CONTINUOUS
ARJUN
How long have you been making my coffee?
ANANYA
Almost six months.
ARJUN
(shocked)
Six months? And I never saw your face?
ANANYA
You saw something.
ARJUN
What?
She quietly looks down at her hands resting on the wooden table.
ANANYA
My hands.
ARJUN
(realizing)
I always noticed the coffee... But I never looked up to notice the person who made it.
A reflective moment of silence falls between them.
SCENE 5
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — CONTINUOUS
Arjun takes an appreciative sip of his coffee and looks directly into Ananya's eyes.
ARJUN
Can I ask you something? Why did you remember my coffee?
Ananya look out across the empty cafe tables, then turns back to him.
ANANYA
Because you were different. Every day, dozens of people rush through that door. They take their cups and disappear. Most don't even acknowledge that we exist.
She points a gentle glance at him.
ANANYA
But you... You always said thank you.
QUICK FLASHBACKS: - DAY 1: Arjun taking his cup blindly, saying "Thank you." - DAY 2: Arjun in his hoodie, walking away saying "Thank you." - DAY 3: Arjun typing intently on his device, uttering "Thank you."
RETURN TO SCENE.
ANANYA
Even when you were completely overwhelmed, even when you were exhausted... You never forgot to appreciate the person making your coffee.
Arjun looks down, visibly moved.
ARJUN
I never realized a small word could mean so much to someone.
ANANYA
Sometimes, the smallest habits show a person's true heart.
SCENE 6
INT. MOONLIGHT COFFEE — NEXT MORNING
The front door opens. DING!
Arjun steps into the cafe. His smartphone is nowhere to be seen—it stays tucked neatly inside his jacket pocket. He looks up immediately, scanning the counter until his eyes lock with Ananya.
ARJUN
Good morning, Ananya.
Ananya pauses, pleasantly surprised.
ANANYA
You remembered my name.
ARJUN
Yesterday made me realize something. For months, I only focused on the coffee. I forgot to notice the person who was making my mornings beautiful.
Ananya beams. She prepares two fresh coffees, brings them over, and slides into the booth right beside him.
This time, Arjun isn't looking at a screen. He looks directly at her.
FINAL SHOT
The camera moves backward through the window pane. Warm morning sunlight streams inside, illuminating the rising steam from their cups. Arjun and Ananya sit together, talking and laughing as the music peaks.
TEXT ON SCREEN: "Sometimes the people who make our ordinary days special are the ones we never stop to notice."
FADE OUT.
THE END
